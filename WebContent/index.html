BLOOD FOR LIFE


Blood.... it runs in our veins and plays a vital role in the body mechanism. But have you ever spared a thought for those who are fighting with some disease and need blood urgently. Act now and donate blood without any worry. It won't cost you a thing but could bring back a smile on someone's face.


WHO NEEDS BLOOD?


Who needs blood.... The need of blood is great. Every year India requires about five crore units of blood to save precious human lives from accidents and burns, heart surgery,organ transplants and patients receiving treatements for leukemia, cancer and other diseases.


AM I ELIGIBLE?


Who can donate..... Donor must be above 18 years of age.Donor should not be suffering from any infectious (such as a cold or flu) or chronic diseases (such as diabetes).You should not have taken any intoxicating drugs, orally or otherwise, within 48 hours prior to donating blood. Donor must wait a minimum of 56 days between whole blood donations. Donor must wait at least 7 days after donating blood before you can donate platelets.
